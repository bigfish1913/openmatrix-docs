## Our Responsibilities

### Community Moderators
Moderators are expected to:
- Use their best judgment to each situation
- Take appropriate action, including warning, temporary ban, or removal from the community
- Report inappropriate behavior to community administrators
Moderators will take the responsibility seriously. We should be taken to ensure a safe and positive environment.

## Enforcement

Moderators may take action at their discretion to including removing content that violates these guidelines.

Moderators have the right and but the discretion, to temporarily ban a user for repeated violations.

 of these guidelines.

- Warning the user: A first-time violation will result in a temporary ban
- Permanent ban, at the discretion of moderators.

### Reporting Issues
Community members are encouraged to report any issues they encounter to community moderators. Users can also report issues directly to community moderators via the appropriate channels, such as:

- GitHub Issues (https://github.com/openmatrix/openmatrix/issues)
- Discord (https://discord.gg/openmatrix)
- Email (mailto: conduct@openmatrix.ai)
